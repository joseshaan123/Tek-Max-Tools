Headquartered in the fast-emerging industrial corridor of Kerala, India, Tek Max Tools operates as our global core powerhouse for high-precision heavy engineering and tool steel moulding fabrication. Our Kerala facility bridges the gap between raw computational metallurgy and high-tolerance material physics, utilizing advanced multi-axis computer numerical control (CNC) work centers to forge durable production components.

By selecting Kerala as our primary technical HQ, we harness an elite regional infrastructure of highly disciplined design engineers, rapid-response tool steel specialists, and direct deep-water maritime logistics routes connecting major global industrial hubs. From this manufacturing foundation, we confidently execute complex, audit-grade tooling lines that support the rigorous production cycles of international automotive OEMs—transforming complex geometric blueprints into highly reliable, long-lifecycle manufacturing assets.
